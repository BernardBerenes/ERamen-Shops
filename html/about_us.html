History

Eramen is the oldest ramen shop in the world since 1910 in Japan and Indonesia since 2015. ERamen is a online ramen shops that serving all ramen with the high quality. We serve around 50.000 bowl per year. Our special ramen is Kurume Ramen that served with pork and any other ingredient.

Vision

ERamen continuously exploring and experimenting with flavors, ingredients, and techniques to create unique and memorable ramen offerings. ERamen also wants that people can love ramen more.

Mission

To bring the delicious and comforting experience of ramen to the digital age. We strive to create innovative and convenient online platforms that allow customers to easily order and enjoy authentic and high- quality ramen from the comfort of their homes.
